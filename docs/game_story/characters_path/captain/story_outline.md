# 🕵️ Captain's Story Outline: Sushil Sinha

## 💂 Perspective & Tone
The Captain is a formal, meticulous, and highly observant Indian officer. He has spent his life mastering British manners to "earn" a place in society, only to find the doors still closed. He views the manor guests with an analytical eye, quickly spotting those who, like himself, are "pretenders" or out of place.

- **Primary Goal**: Expose the Host, Lady Claythorn, as an impostor and survive the manor's hidden dangers.
- **Key Trait**: Observant. He notices inconsistencies in etiquette, genealogy, and military history that others miss.
