# Broken - Sunday Afternoon (The Walk)

The walk from the manor to the village. No menus - the chapter branches
entirely on the `left_together` thread set in the Sunday-morning chapter.

- **Without `left_together`**: Broken and the Captain walk out alone. A mile
  past the tree they are shot down one after the other
  -> `broken_ending_ambushed` (intuition ending: take everyone with you).
- **With `left_together`**: all six walk out in a column, help one another
  over the tree, and reach the police station by late afternoon
  -> `broken_ending_walked_out` (the final ending).

## setup_broken_sunday_afternoon_1.json
`left_together` not unlocked. The two-man walk -> `broken_ending_ambushed`.

## setup_broken_sunday_afternoon_2.json
`left_together` unlocked. The party walks out together ->
`broken_ending_walked_out`.
